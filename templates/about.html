About NeuroScan AI

Revolutionizing brain tumor detection with artificial intelligence

Our Mission

NeuroScan AI is dedicated to providing accurate, fast, and accessible brain tumor detection using cutting-edge deep learning technology. Our mission is to assist medical professionals in early diagnosis and improve patient outcomes through AI-powered analysis.

How It Works

Upload MRI Scan

Medical professionals upload brain MRI scans through our secure platform.

AI Analysis

Our deep learning model analyzes the scan for tumor presence and type with high accuracy.

Get Results

Receive immediate results with detailed classification and confidence levels.

Our Technology

Deep Learning Model

Our system uses a state-of-the-art EfficientNetB0 model trained on thousands of brain MRI scans to detect and classify tumors with high accuracy.

  • Detects glioma, meningioma, pituitary tumors, and normal scans
  • Accuracy of over 95% on validation datasets
  • Processes images in seconds for rapid diagnosis

Data Security

We prioritize patient privacy and data security in all our operations.

  • HIPAA-compliant data handling procedures
  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Automatic deletion of uploaded images after processing

Intended Use

NeuroScan AI is intended for use by qualified healthcare professionals as a decision support tool. It is not a substitute for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a physician for medical advice about any diagnostic findings.
