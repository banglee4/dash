Our Research Methodology

NeuroScan AI's technology is built on rigorous scientific research and continuous improvement. Our team of data scientists and medical experts collaborate to develop the most accurate brain tumor detection algorithms.

Dataset

  • Curated dataset of over 3,000 brain MRI scans
  • Balanced distribution across 4 classes: Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors, and No tumor
  • Annotated by board-certified radiologists

Model Architecture

  • EfficientNetB0 architecture with transfer learning
  • Custom classification head with dropout regularization
  • Optimized for both accuracy and computational efficiency
